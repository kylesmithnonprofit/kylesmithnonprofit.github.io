I'm Kyle Smith, an [Assistant Professor](https://www.business.msstate.edu/directory/kas1205) in the Adkerson School of Accountancy at Mississippi State University. I received my Ph.D. in Accounting from the University of Alabama under Dr. Linda Parsons. Prior to my Ph.D., I spent two years with Deloitte, working on audit readiness and financial reporting for a DoD agency.

My research agenda focuses on accounting questions within the nonprofit setting, primarily related to donor giving decisions, organization management and decision making, and private foundations. My passion for research is driven by my love for the nonprofit sector - I am a huge believer in the power of philanthropy and the change that nonprofit organizations can make in the world. Ultimately, I strive to conduct research that has a positive impact on the sector.

At MSU, I teach Accounting Analytics (undergraduate) and Advanced Accounting Analytics (graduate). Both courses I designed from the ground up and cover material I am very passionate about. Systems II was originally designed to be an Excel skills course, and I cover core Excel skills, Power Query, Power BI, and Large Language Models. Accounting Data Analytics is functionally an introduction to data science, and I cover data management and machine learning techniques in R, with a heavy emphasis on using LLM to accelerate student coding abilities. Over the last two years, I have been incorporating AI into research and course content. I am a huge believer in the disruptive potential of AI, and I have made it a point to incorporate it into my research (I have a working paper that uses LLM as a classifier), and teaching. 

See above tabs for information on my Research, Teaching, and other notes. Feel free to reach out by email for anything!
