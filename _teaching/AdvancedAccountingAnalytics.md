Functioning as an introduction to data science for accountants, this graduate course dives deeper into data management, classification, and regression techniques using R. To ensure we spend our class time effectively, I incorporate LLMs to handle much of the R syntax work, which allows us to focus more heavily on the overarching analytics and machine learning concepts. I also enjoy bringing my research passion into the classroom by utilizing nonprofit accounting data for assignments and exams, giving students experience with rich, real-world datasets.
