This course introduces the essential tools needed to complete common data analytic tasks in the accounting profession. The curriculum is highly practical, focusing on both fundamental and advanced concepts in Excel, Power Query, and Power BI. Recognizing the rapid changes in the industry, I conclude the semester with a four-week module on Large Language Models (LLMs). Students learn how to effectively use LLMs to solve problems through prompt engineering, vibe coding, and other practical applications. The course culminates in a collaborative final project where students must develop a unique goal and execute an end-to-end data analysis on a unique dataset.
